Frequently Asked Questions (Draft)

This draft FAQ page is intended to answer common questions about sessions, scheduling, and pricing. Specific wording and policies will be finalized with the client.

How long does a typical session last?

Most family and kids’ sessions are scheduled for 45–60 minutes, depending on the package.

What should we wear for our session?

Coordinated colors, simple patterns, and comfortable outfits usually photograph best.

Do you offer digital files and prints?

Packages generally include digital images with the option to order prints and albums.

How do military discounts work?

Eligible families can select from specially marked packages. See the Military Discounts page for conceptual details.
