Frequently Asked Questions

Here are answers to some of the most common questions about scheduling, sessions, and photography packages at Dinapicso Photography.

How long does a typical session last?

Most family and kids’ sessions last between 45 and 60 minutes, depending on the selected package and session goals.

What should we wear for our session?

Coordinated colors, simple patterns, and comfortable outfits tend to photograph best. We recommend avoiding large logos or busy prints.

Do you offer digital files and prints?

Yes. Packages include professionally edited digital images with optional print and album packages available.

How do military discounts work?

Active-duty, reserve, and veteran families are eligible for special discounted packages. Please visit the Military Discounts & Packages page for details.
